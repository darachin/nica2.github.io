Overview

AThe National Institute of Civil Aviation of the Secretariat of State for Civil Aviation is a specialized institution for the training of human resources in the field of civil aviation, which received a grant from the Republic of Korea through the International Cooperation Agency (KOICA) in October 2013. Civil Aviation in Cambodia is better and more efficient through human resource training for:

- Contribute to the maintenance and training of human resources in the field of civil aviation to ensure security, safety and quality in the field of domestic and international air transport in accordance with the standards of the International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO).

- To strengthen and expand human resource capacity and efficiency in the management of the civil aviation sector in the Kingdom of Cambodia.

The training at the National Institute of Civil Aviation focuses on the use of key equipment in the civil aviation sector, such as ATC Simulator, DME, ILS, X-Ray. And important courses that are in line with the skills of civil aviation officials and aviation staff in order to meet the objectives of the Secretariat of Civil Aviation and to participate in strengthening human resource capacity in the field of civil aviation in Cambodia.

Vision

The Civil Aviation Training Center will be the best training institution in building human resource capacity for the civil aviation sector in the region.

Mission

- Strive to prepare training strategies for the Cambodian civil aviation sector with professionalism and independence, and make all aspects of civil aviation in line with the needs of customers and society by reviewing quality and safety standards.

- Provide development in civil aviation training based on competence, talent, to create the best international value.

Values

- Excellence:
We seek results that demonstrate accountability, transparency and professionalism.

- Competitiveness:
We do our best to reach our goals successfully..

- Commitment:
We will provide the best quality of service and training with safety as our priority.
